About Us

The Archbishop Benjamin Nzimbi Foundation (ABNF) is a christian-based organization dedicated to community transformation through discipleship, capacity building, and sustainable development. Rooted in Christian values, ABNF believes in empowering individuals, families, and institutions to grow spiritually, socially, and economically.

Our approach is guided by biblical principles such as Luke 14:28â€“30 and Matthew 13:32, encouraging communities to start small, use local resources, and grow into fully empowered and self-sustaining entities.

Our Vision

To see a developed and transformed community enjoying life in its fullness.

Our Mission

To equip communities with diverse skills for improved livelihoods, through capacity building and training.

Our Core Values

  • We are Christian
  • We value life
  • We believe in family
  • We value integrity and justice
  • We value equality of opportunities
  • We value the environment

Who We Serve

ABNF serves communities, families, and institutions. We support widows, orphans, youth, and the elderly, empower families through parenting, and train institutions in leadership, planning, and governance.

Our Structure

ABNF is governed by a professional board, guided by a secretariat, and supported by focal points in each of our strategic pillars: Education, Health, WASH (Water supply, Saitation and Hygiene Promotion), Food Security, Youth Development, Chaplaincy, and Peace & Justice.

Gratitude
