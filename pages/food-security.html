Food Security & Livelihood

Overview:
ABNF supports communities in building food resilience through sustainable agriculture, agribusiness, and innovative farming practices that improve household nutrition and incomes.

Strategic Direction:

  • Promote alternative food production systems and diversified farming.
  • Enhance agro-entrepreneurship and value addition among smallholder farmers.
  • Provide access to farming inputs and training for improved productivity.

Key Activities:

  • Train farmers on climate-smart agriculture and post-harvest handling.
  • Distribute seeds, farming kits, and livestock starter packs.
  • Link youth to agri-business mentorship and apprenticeships.
  • Support value chain development and market access initiatives.
