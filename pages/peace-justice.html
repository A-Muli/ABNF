Peace & Justice

Overview:
ABNF promotes peaceful coexistence, justice, and social harmony through conflict resolution, civic education, and mediation initiatives.

Strategic Direction:

  • Strengthen conflict resolution and reconciliation efforts.
  • Foster interfaith dialogue and mutual respect.
  • Promote human rights awareness and legal literacy.

Key Activities:

  • Community peace forums and civic education workshops.
  • Support mediation and arbitration efforts at local level.
  • Train community justice advocates.

Archbishop Benjamin Nzimbi Foundation

ABNF is a Christian-based organization dedicated to the holistic transformation of communities through education, leadership, mentorship, and sustainable development.
