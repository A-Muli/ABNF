Chaplaincy

Overview:
ABNF provides spiritual and psychosocial support to individuals in schools, prisons, institutions, and public service through its chaplaincy outreach. The goal is to promote mental health, moral values, and faith in God among youth, professionals, and families.

Strategic Direction:

  • Offer institutional chaplaincy, prayer, and counseling support.
  • Promote mental wellness, spiritual formation, and responsible leadership.
  • Provide outreach and discipleship to vulnerable and underrepresented populations.

Key Activities:

  • Chaplaincy seminars in schools, prisons, and government offices.
  • Annual prayer day for leaders, professionals, and families.
  • Discipleship programs and youth mentorship through scripture-based sessions.
  • Establish a helpline for counseling and prayer requests.
