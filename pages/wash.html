WASH (Water, Sanitation & Hygiene)

Overview:
ABNF promotes sustainable access to clean water, proper sanitation, and hygiene education — especially in underserved rural communities.

Strategic Direction:

  • Improve household access to clean water.
  • Promote hygiene through community education.
  • Build and maintain sustainable water systems.

Key Activities:

  • Drill boreholes and build shallow wells.
  • Construct latrines and handwashing stations in schools.
  • Train Water User Committees.
  • Sanitation awareness campaigns.

Potential Partners:

  • Ministry of Water, Sanitation and Irrigation
  • KITWASCO & KIMWASCO
  • Living Water International Kenya
  • Life in Abundance International
