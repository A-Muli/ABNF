Youth Development & Mentorship

Overview:
ABNF invests in youth as key agents of transformation through leadership development, entrepreneurship, and mentorship programs.

Strategic Direction:

  • Equip youth with practical and entrepreneurial skills.
  • Promote moral and leadership development through mentorship.
  • Strengthen youth participation in community development.

Key Activities:

  • Youth leadership camps and mentorship clubs.
  • Entrepreneurship training and business incubators.
  • Counseling and psychosocial support sessions.

Potential Partners:

  • Kenya Youth Empowerment Project (KYEP)
  • Church youth ministries and networks
  • Enterprise incubators and training institutions
