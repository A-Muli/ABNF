Education

Overview:
ABNF is committed to ensuring children and youth in Kitui County access inclusive, quality education. Through mentorship, infrastructure support, and school-based programs, the foundation empowers learners for a brighter future rooted in Christian values.

Strategic Direction:

  • Improve access to inclusive basic and tertiary education for vulnerable children and youth.
  • Support church-owned schools in infrastructure and curriculum development.
  • Incorporate ICT, innovation, and life skills training in schools.
  • Mentor students to build resilience, leadership, and values-based living.

Key Activities:

  • School fee support for children and youth (primary, secondary, tertiary).
  • Mentorship and coaching programs targeting learners and teachers.
  • Construction and equipping of classrooms, labs, and libraries.
  • Training and capacity building for Parent-Teacher Associations (PTAs).
