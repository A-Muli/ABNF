Health

Overview:
ABNF responds to the health needs of communities by promoting accessible, affordable healthcare, with a focus on mental health, gender-based violence (GBV), and community wellness through a Christian lens.

Strategic Direction:

  • Promote mental health awareness and psychosocial wellness.
  • Support primary health care services, especially maternal and child health.
  • Strengthen responses to GBV, including prevention and survivor support.

Key Activities:

  • Health outreaches including deworming, immunization, and nutrition services.
  • Capacity building on community-based maternal and child care.
  • Community awareness forums on mental health and emotional resilience.
  • Trainings on GBV awareness, legal rights, and survivor support systems.

Potential Partners:

  • Ministry of Health
  • PATH
  • Pathfinder International
  • Local hospitals and VCT centers
