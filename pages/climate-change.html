Climate Change & Environmental Stewardship

Overview:
ABNF promotes environmental care and climate resilience through community awareness, sustainable agriculture, and conservation practices.

Strategic Direction:

  • Build community resilience to climate change impacts.
  • Promote environmental stewardship and green practices.
  • Support climate-smart agricultural innovations.

Key Activities:

  • Tree planting drives and environmental clean-ups.
  • Train farmers on regenerative and organic agriculture.
  • Establish community eco-clubs and green hubs.
