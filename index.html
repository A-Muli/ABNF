About Us

Archbishop Nzimbi Foundation (ABNF) is a Christian-based organization dedicated to the transformation of communities, families, and institutions through skill-building, leadership development, and sustainable development programs

Vision & Mission

Our core is rooted in transforming lives through empowerment, education, leadership, and love. We believe a thriving society starts with equipping individuals and families with the right tools and values. .

Vision

Mission

  • To see a developed and transformed community enjoying life in its fullness.
  • To equip communities with diverse skills for improved livelihoods through capacity building, training, mentorship, and sustainable development programs

Our Strategic Objectives

Education

Empowering through knowledge and opportunity.

Water Supply, Sanitation and Hygiene Provision (WASH)

Improving health through clean water and sanitation.

Food Security

Creating sustainable livelihoods and nutrition access.

Chaplaincy

Ministering hope, healing, and spiritual growth.

Health

Strengthening community wellness and access to care.

Peace & Justice

Building harmony, conflict resolution, and advocacy.

Youth Development

Nurturing talents, leadership, and mentorship in youth.

Climate Change

Championing environmental sustainability and resilience.

Core Values

  • We value equality of opportunities
  • We value the environment.
