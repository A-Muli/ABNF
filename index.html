About Us

Archbishop Nzimbi Foundation (ABNF) is a Christian-based organization dedicated to the holistic transformation of communities, families, and institutions through skill-building, leadership development, and sustainable development programs

Vision & Mission

Our core is rooted in transforming lives through empowerment, education, leadership, and love. We believe a thriving society starts with equipping individuals and families with the right tools and values. .

Vision

Mission

  • To see a developed and transformed community enjoying life in its fullness..
  • To equip communities with diverse skills for improved livelihoods through capacity building, training, mentorship, and sustainable development programs

Core Values

  • We are Christian,
  • We value life,
  • We believe in family,
  • We value integrity and justice,
  • We value equality of opportunities
  • We value the environment.

Objectives

  1. Enhance access to education, food, health, water, sanitation, environment, peace, and justice.
  2. Train families and communities in transformational leadership and development.
  3. Mentor leaders and institutions for sustainability and character development.
  4. Promote intergenerational dialogue for community well-being.
  5. Establish a fund for grants and donations to support vulnerable groups.
